Coming to Steam

Mind Over Mushroom - a turn-based tactical strategy game currently in development.

  • Challenging turn-based combat. Approach each battle with your own strategy.
  • Free-form, non-grid based movement system. Make your moves freely, based on distance instead of the squares of a grid.
  • Fight your way though 25+ tactical battles set in a variety of diverse locations, from dark caves and dry deserts, to dense jungles and desolate wastelands.
  • Over a dozen unique character types to unlock, each with their own abilities.
  • Focused purely on strategy. No "RPG elements" to distract from the strategy of battle. Individual characters are not persistent from one battle to the next and do not level up or acquire equipment. You begin each battle from a clean slate, choosing your forces from the available character types you've unlocked to best suit the tactical situation.
